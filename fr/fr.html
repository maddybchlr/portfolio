First Readers

Books, 5 x 5"
2019

a series of three children's style board books which explore the fears and anxieties involved in growing up and taking on the responsibilities of an adult. The concept and illustration style was influenced by Konrad Lorenz's concept of kindchenschema, or infantile features which motivate care-taking behaviors in older individuals. As the characters become more distressed, their features become more infantile representing the characters' desire retain their innocence or keep being protected from discomfort and captures the idea of feeling like a child in an adult world.

Six copies were exhibited during Cake Walk on November 1, 2019 in Richmond, VA.

Handle With Care

Publishing Imprint
2019

a publishing imprint which explores the topics of fragility, sensitivity, and vulnerability through various mediums and print formats. It explores these topics in informal, abstracted compositions through the eyes of fragile souls. Photography and illustration are thoughtfully applied in order to capture the process of exploring and coming to understand these ideas. Handle With Care places great value on education, self-discovery, and self-acceptance while pursuing a better understanding of these traits and the roles they play in our lives and culture. It celebrates both the advantages and the disadvantages associated and asks that others do the same.

Featured projects include a series of three, 5 x 5" books and an 11 x 17" risograph poster among others. Handle With Care displayed a table during publishing fairs Sight, Site, Cite and And We're Not Dead... Yet in 2019.
